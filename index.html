The trends over the last several decades have shown increased abnormal weather patterns. Hail storms are an example of this. There is more conclusive data pointing to the fact of climate change but small observations, such as these, make an over all point in the accumulation of scientific consensus on climate change being a reality. Years selected on the slider below will show hail storm frequency in the given year over the last 70 years.
